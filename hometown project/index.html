Visit Aburi

Enjoy Nature in this vibrant district in the Eastern Region of Ghana.

Top three activities to do at Aburi

Visit the Aburi botanical gardens

The Aburi botanical showcasing Diversity of global flora. Perfect for picnic, relaxing and unique local refreshment.

Go for quad biking in the mountains at ATV Aburi

ATV Aburi is the perfect place to escape from the hustle and bustle of city life, offering an exciting and adrenaline-pumping adventure in the heart of the countryside.

Go for a hike and swim at the Adom waterfalls

The falls has been a destination for hiking, swimming and mountain bike excursions.
